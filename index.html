About Us

INTERNATIONAL FOREIGN LANGUAGE SCHOOL is a dream endeavour of its mother organization, Golden Horizon for People Trust, which is a non-governmental charitable social organization . The school started its journey on 20th August'17 with only 5 students in French language. After that School started gradually Spanish from 1st June 2018 with 6 students. Thereafter, on 13th October 2019 German, on August- 2022 Japanese with 10 students. Now we are conducting our school with French, German, Spanish, Spoken English ( IELTS preparation) and Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Italian, Chinese (Mandarin) languages . Previously it has been definitely a huge troublesome journey as the school never compromises in the learning excellency as the school mainly guides and nourishes the students to secure their goal after having the respective International certificates from different Foreign Countries. The school doesn't deny its basic promise and that is allowing the students who are mostly from rural and suburb and are mostly from below income group. Our moto is to give the opportunity to these students to learn these languages and secure a job. Apart from class study, the school and its excellent team of faculties encourage all students to be acquainted with the culture of that country through film and different cultural programs. Once in a year we use to organize cultural seminar on different languages where the students get the opportunity to perform on stage.

From The desk of Director

I have joined this organization on 1st July 2017 as the Director of this institute (IFLS). I can proudly say that International Foreign Language School is a unique educational institute which provides opportunities to the students to learn foreign languages with a nominal fees and inspire younger generation to make their career in this field. We also make the students aware about its job opportunities and its advantages in today's world. Till now 10 students from our Institution are working in different states , in different eminent MNCS' and also in international Schools. Apart from class study we encourage our students for cultural research work on their chosen foreign languages.

Dr. Sachidra Nath Maiti
